Snackbar
Snackbars are often used as tooltips/popups to show a message at the bottom of the screen.
Snackbar with Text only:
Can't send photo. Retry in 5 seconds.
                            
<div class="snack-bar-wrapper">
    <div class="snack-bar fixed-bottom show">
        <div class="snack-bar-content">
            Can't send photo. Retry in 5 seconds.
        </div>
    </div>
</div>
Snackbar with Text and Action:
Can't send photo. Retry in 5 seconds.
                            
<div class="component-content-section
fixed bg-charcoal-white">
    <div class="snack-bar-wrapper">
        <div class="snack-bar fixed-bottom show">
            <div class="snack-bar-content">
                Can't send photo. Retry in 5 seconds.
            </div>
            <div class="snack-bar-action">
                <button 
                    class="btn btn-sm white
                        bg-charcoal-black">
                    RETRY
                </button>
                <button
                    class="btn btn-round 
                        white bg-charcoal-black">
                    <i class="material-icons">
                        close
                    </i>
                </button>
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
</div>
